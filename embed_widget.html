🔌 Embed Widget

Put "Book Now" on any website

One script tag. Your customers see a floating booking button wherever they find you online. Works on any website — Squarespace, Wix, WordPress, or plain HTML.

How to install
1
Copy the snippet
Click the copy button below to grab your personalized script tag with your Shop ID already filled in.
2
Paste before </body>
In your website editor, open the HTML and paste the snippet just before the closing body tag. That's it.
3
Done — you're live
A "Book Now" button appears on your site within seconds. Customers can request appointments without calling.
Your Embed Snippet
HTML — paste before </body> 
<script
  src="https://ringdesk.ai/widget.js"
  data-shop="YOUR_SHOP_ID"
  data-color="#7c3aed"
  data-position="bottom-left"
  data-label="Book Now"
></script>

What the widget does

Booking requests
Customers enter name, phone, service, and preferred time. Requests go straight to your RingDesk dashboard.

After submitting, your AI texts the customer to confirm details — just like a phone booking.
