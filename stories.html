Carlos Méndez had been cutting hair in Little Havana for eleven years. Business was good — too good, actually. He was booked solid most days, which meant every time his phone rang while he had someone in the chair, it went to voicemail. Most of those callers never called back.

Prestige Cuts
Bronx, New York · 2 barbers + owner
Pro Plan
Three barbers, one AI. They stopped fighting over who answers the phone.
Marcus and his two barbers at Prestige Cuts in the South Bronx had an unofficial system: whoever wasn't in the middle of a cut answered the phone. It led to constant interruptions, dropped clippers, and frustrated clients in the chair.

"It was chaos. Someone would be mid-fade and just grab the phone because nobody else could. The client in the chair had to wait, the caller got a distracted answer, and half the time the booking never even made it to the book," Marcus explained.

After setting up RingDesk, the phone problem disappeared entirely. The AI handles every call. All three barbers stay focused. The booking calendar fills automatically. Marcus checks the dashboard each morning to see what came in overnight.

"The overnight bookings alone paid for RingDesk in the first week. We were getting calls at 11pm from people trying to book for the weekend. We had no idea. Now those bookings just appear in the morning."

— Marcus Thompson, Owner, Prestige Cuts

Diego Reyes runs La Barbería Reyes in Pilsen — Chicago's largest Mexican-American neighborhood. About 60% of his clients speak primarily Spanish. His previous answering service was English-only, which meant half his callers hung up confused or couldn't book at all.

"I tried a different AI service first. English only. My Spanish-speaking clients called, heard the English menu, and hung up. I was literally losing customers because of the phone system," Diego said.

With RingDesk, the AI detects Spanish in the first sentence and switches the entire conversation — booking, confirmation, everything — into Spanish. No menus, no "press 2," just natural conversation.
