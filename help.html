Everything you need to know about setting up and using RingDesk.

How long does setup take?
About 15 minutes. You enter your shop name, hours, and services. Then forward your phone number to the RingDesk number we give you — that's it. Most owners are live before their next appointment. No technical knowledge needed.
Does RingDesk replace my phone number?
No — your number stays yours. RingDesk works via call forwarding. You keep your existing number and forward it to RingDesk when you're busy. Turn forwarding on or off any time from your phone settings. If you cancel, your number works exactly as before.
What happens if the AI can't answer a question?
If a caller asks something outside the AI's knowledge, it politely says: "Let me have the owner reach out to you directly." It logs the caller's number and reason in your dashboard as a pending callback. Nothing falls through the cracks.
Can clients book with a specific barber?
Yes, if you configure it. In your settings, add barbers or stylists by name. When a client says "I want to book with Marcus," the AI recognizes the name and books with that person's schedule. If unavailable, it offers the next available slot.
Does it work for nail salons and spas too?
Yes — any appointment-based shop. RingDesk works for barbershops, salons, nail studios, lash studios, spas, and threading bars. The AI understands service names for each type. You define the service list; the AI learns it automatically.
What languages does the AI speak?
English and Spanish out of the box — the AI detects the caller's language automatically and responds in kind. No setup, no extra cost. Portuguese, Vietnamese, Korean, and Mandarin are in development and will be added to all plans at no charge.
Is there a setup fee?
No setup fee, ever. You pay the monthly plan rate and nothing else. No onboarding fee, no porting fee, no hardware required. If you need help, our support team will walk you through it on a 15-minute screen share — also free.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — no contracts, no commitment. Cancel from your dashboard any time before your next billing date and you won't be charged again. Your data is available for export for 30 days after cancellation, then deleted per our privacy policy.
How do I get my money back if it doesn't work?
14-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. If you're not satisfied within 14 days of your first payment, email support@ringdesk.ai and we'll refund 100% of your first month.
Does it integrate with my existing booking software?
Google Calendar sync is on the roadmap for Q3 2026, followed by Square Appointments. Right now, RingDesk maintains its own appointment calendar you can view in the dashboard and export to CSV. Let us know which integration you need — we prioritize by demand.
