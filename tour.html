Natural conversation. 0.4 second response time.
When a client calls, your AI picks up in under a second. The conversation flows naturally — the AI asks for their name, what service they want, preferred day and time. It confirms the booking, mentions the reminders, and hangs up. Total call time: under 60 seconds.
  • Answers in English and Spanish automatically
  • Books, reschedules, or cancels appointments
  • Handles Q&A (hours, prices, location)
  • Transfers urgent calls to your cell phone

03 / SMS Confirmations
Confirmation text in 10 seconds. Reminders automatically.
The moment a booking is confirmed, your client gets a text with the appointment details, your address, and a Google Maps link. They also get a reminder the day before and two hours before — reducing no-shows by 67%.
  • Instant confirmation SMS with address + Maps link
  • 24-hour reminder ("See you tomorrow!")
  • 2-hour reminder ("You're booked for 2pm today")
  • Day 3 + Day 7 follow-up to rebook
