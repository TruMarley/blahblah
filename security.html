Data Encryption

All data stored in RingDesk — appointments, phone numbers, call logs — is encrypted at rest using AES-256. Data in transit between your phone carrier, our servers, and your dashboard uses TLS 1.3. Encryption keys are rotated quarterly and never stored alongside data.

AES-256 at rest · TLS 1.3 in transit
📱

TCPA Compliance

RingDesk's SMS reminders qualify under the Established Business Relationship (EBR) exemption — clients who book an appointment have an existing relationship with your shop. Every outbound SMS includes opt-out instructions ("Reply STOP to unsubscribe") and opt-outs are honored immediately and permanently. We do not send marketing SMS to opted-out numbers, ever.

EBR exemption · Immediate opt-out processing
🎙️

Call Recording Policy

RingDesk does not record calls by default. Audio is streamed in real time for transcription and immediately discarded — no recordings are stored on our servers. If call recording is enabled (opt-in feature, not yet available), calls will be stored encrypted and customers will hear a disclosure at the start of the call as required by applicable law.

Not recorded by default
🏅

SOC 2 Roadmap

We are actively working toward SOC 2 Type I certification, planned for Q3 2026. Our controls cover security, availability, and confidentiality trust service criteria. We work with a certified third-party auditor and will publish the report summary upon completion.

Type I — Q3 2026 target
🌍

GDPR & CCPA

Customers have the right to request deletion of their data at any time. RingDesk processes deletion requests within 30 days. For GDPR purposes, you (the shop owner) are the data controller and RingDesk is the processor — we provide a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) on request. For CCPA, we do not sell personal data to third parties.

Deletion within 30 days · DPA available · No data sales
🛡️

Penetration Testing

RingDesk undergoes annual penetration testing by an independent third-party security firm. Tests cover our API endpoints, authentication flows, WebSocket connections, and Twilio webhook handlers. Critical findings are patched within 24 hours; high-severity findings within 7 days. Results are available to enterprise customers under NDA.

Annual third-party pentest · 24h critical patch SLA
