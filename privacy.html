Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 2026  ·  Effective date: January 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how RingDesk, Inc. ("RingDesk," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you and your customers when you use the RingDesk AI receptionist service. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to transparency about our data practices.

Section 1

Information We Collect

Account information: When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, business name, phone number, and billing information. This information is required to provide the Service.

Call data: When your business phone receives calls through RingDesk, we process call audio in real time. Call transcripts are generated by our speech recognition provider and stored in your account. Call metadata (duration, timestamp, caller phone number, outcome) is stored in your dashboard database.

Appointment data: The AI collects caller names, phone numbers, and appointment details (service, date, time, barber preference) during the booking process. This data is stored in your shop's appointment database.

Usage data: We automatically collect information about how you interact with the RingDesk dashboard, including pages visited, features used, and session duration. This helps us improve the product.

Your customers' data: Your customers' names, phone numbers, appointment history, and any information they share during calls is processed through RingDesk on your behalf. You are the data controller for your customers' data; RingDesk acts as a data processor.

Section 2

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and improve the AI receptionist service
  • Book appointments and send SMS confirmations and reminders on your behalf
  • Generate analytics and performance reports for your dashboard
  • Send you billing receipts, account notices, and product updates
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

We use call transcripts and aggregated anonymized interaction data to improve our AI models' understanding of barbershop and salon booking scenarios. Individual call recordings are not shared with other customers or used to train models in ways that could identify your business or your customers.

We do not sell your personal information or your customers' personal information to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. We will not use your customers' phone numbers to contact them for any purpose other than providing the Service you have configured.

Section 3

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on servers located in the United States. We use industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS 1.3), encryption at rest for sensitive data, access controls, and regular security audits. Our SQLite appointment database is stored locally on your server instance; our hosted infrastructure uses encrypted cloud storage.

We retain call metadata and transcripts for 90 days by default. Appointment records are retained for the duration of your subscription plus 30 days following termination. You may request earlier deletion at any time by contacting privacy@ringdesk.ai.

Despite our security measures, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data. If we become aware of a security breach affecting your account, we will notify you within 72 hours in compliance with applicable law.

Section 4

Third-Party Services

RingDesk integrates with the following third-party services to deliver the AI receptionist functionality. Each has its own privacy practices:

Twilio (twilio.com)
Handles telephone call routing and SMS delivery. Twilio processes call audio and phone numbers. Subject to Twilio's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
Deepgram (deepgram.com)
Provides real-time speech-to-text transcription (Nova-2 model) and text-to-speech voice synthesis (Aura model). Call audio is sent to Deepgram's API for processing. Subject to Deepgram's Privacy Policy.
Anthropic (anthropic.com)
Provides the Claude AI model used for natural language understanding and booking intent detection. Call transcripts are sent to Anthropic's API for processing. Subject to Anthropic's Usage Policy and Privacy Policy.
Stripe (stripe.com)
Processes subscription billing and payment information. RingDesk does not store complete credit card numbers. Subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy.
Google (google.com) — Optional
If you connect Google Calendar, appointment data is synced to your Google Calendar via the Google Calendar API using a service account you authorize. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
Section 5

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active and for 30 days following account termination to allow for data export. Call transcripts and recordings are retained for 90 days. Appointment records and customer contact information are retained for the life of your subscription.

Anonymous, aggregated data derived from your usage may be retained indefinitely for service improvement purposes. This data cannot be used to identify you or your customers.

You may request deletion of your account data at any time. Certain data may need to be retained longer to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements. We will inform you of any such retention when processing your deletion request.

Section 6

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA): the right to know what personal information we collect and how it is used; the right to request deletion of your personal information; the right to opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information); and the right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights.

To exercise your CCPA rights, submit a verifiable consumer request to privacy@ringdesk.ai. We will respond within 45 days. You may designate an authorized agent to make requests on your behalf by providing written authorization.

Section 7

GDPR Rights (EEA and UK)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR): the right to access your personal data; the right to rectification of inaccurate data; the right to erasure ("right to be forgotten"); the right to restriction of processing; the right to data portability; and the right to object to processing based on legitimate interests.

Our legal basis for processing your personal data is: contract performance (to provide the Service you subscribed to), legitimate interests (to improve our service and prevent fraud), and consent (for optional data uses such as marketing emails). You may withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

For cross-border data transfers from the EEA to the United States, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission. To exercise your GDPR rights or for data protection inquiries, contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@ringdesk.ai.
