How to read this: Each row is a group of clients who booked their first appointment in that month ("cohort"). The columns show what % of them came back 1, 2, and 3 months later.

What's healthy: M1 retention of 70%+ means most clients re-book. M3 retention of 60%+ is exceptional for a service business — industry average is 45–55%. If M1 drops below 60%, investigate: were SMS reminders sent? Was the service quality consistent?

Empty cells (—) mean not enough time has passed to measure that month's cohort.
