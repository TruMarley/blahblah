## Instagram DM Templates (Barbershop Owners)

**Rules before sending:**
- Like 2-3 of their recent posts before DMing (warms them up, boosts response rate from 5% → 24%)
- Keep under 100 words
- Lead with their specific situation — not your product
- One CTA only: "Want to see it?"

---

### DM #1 — The Missed Call Hook (highest converter)

> Hey [Name]! Love the shop — those fades are clean 🔥
>
> Quick question: how many calls do you miss while you're mid-cut? I built an AI that answers every call 24/7 and books the appointment automatically. Already set up for a few shops in [City].
>
> Want to see a 30-second demo?

---

> I made an AI receptionist that answers those — even at midnight — and books them straight into your calendar. $99/month, no contracts.

## TikTok Video Concepts

**Best performing format for SaaS + small biz:** 15-30s, POV or "reaction," trending audio, CAPTION the hook in the first frame.

---

### TikTok #1 — "I called a barbershop and an AI answered 🤯" (POV Caller)

**Hook (first 3 seconds, text overlay):** "I called a random barbershop… and THIS happened"

**Script:**
> [Shows phone, dials a barbershop]
> [AI voice answers naturally]
> AI: "Thanks for calling Cuts & Fades! I'm Alex, your AI assistant. How can I help?"
> [Caller books appointment in 30 seconds]
> [Reaction face — jaw drop]
> Text overlay: "They never missed a call. It booked my appointment automatically. And then TEXTED me the confirmation."
> End card: "This is RingDesk. $99/month. barbershops are going crazy for this."

**Why it works:** Unexpected reveal, authentic reaction, shows the product working in real life. Expected 100K-500K views.

---

### TikTok #2 — "POV: You're a barber and you never miss a booking again"

**Hook:** "POV: your phone answers itself while you're cutting 📞✂️"

**Script:**
> [Split screen: barber cutting hair on left, phone screen on right]
> Phone rings. Left screen: barber keeps cutting. Right screen: AI picks up.
> AI: "Thanks for calling! Want to book an appointment?"
> Caller: "Yeah, Saturday at 10?"
> AI: "Booked! Texting you the confirmation now."
> [Barber looks up, phone notification: "New booking — Saturday 10am"]
> Text: "Never lose a booking again. RingDesk AI."

**Why it works:** Addresses exact pain point, shows outcome not just feature, relatable to every barber.

---

### TikTok #3 — "The math doesn't lie" (Talking head / finance style)

**Hook:** "Barbers lose $450/week to missed calls. Here's the math 🧮"

**Script:**
> "If you miss 5 calls a week — which is LOW — and each one is a $90 ticket…"
> "That's $450 gone. Every week. $23,000 a year."
> "I built an AI that answers every call for $99 a month."
> "That's $1,188 a year to capture $23,000."
> "That's a 1,800% ROI."
> "Still waiting to answer your phone?"
> [Screen record: AI booking an appointment in real time]

**Why it works:** Finance/ROI content crushes on TikTok right now. Specific numbers = credibility.
